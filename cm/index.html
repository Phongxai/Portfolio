Creative

We are Creative

Creativity plays a big role in most if not all fields. Without it, we lack vision, innovation and change.

Below is an example of my creativity:

My Task? To redesign an certain aspects of the organizations website. While going through their old site I notice a few things. Their original color scheme didn't represent who they were and each page contained very little information. Instead of doing design snippets, I suggested that we change the whole look and feel of their new site. Almost as if we were doing a rebranding of their organization, but not really (If that makes sense). Their mission will be the same. Just the look and feel of their website will be different.

My Goal? A better experience for users and a color scheme that would better fit their organization.

My idea? A one page website that would tell a story.

My Outcome? The organization liked my idea and decided to go with my design.

Helping

We love to Help

I believe the most important aspect of helping is building relationships. Establishing good relationships is key to any project, team or company. Bridging people together is the start of success.

Recently, I have been volunteering at Edison High School to help students design and prototype their web application. The students are part a program called Junior Achievement. This program aims to inspire the next generation of young entrepreneurs by having them plan and start their own business. Their business was a student rewards program.

My role? To help guide and teach the students how to plan, design and build a prototype of their web application.

My Goal? Inspire the students to have an entrepreneurial spirit and give them an introduction to the world of digital. To the left is an early low fidelity wireframe I had the students do.
