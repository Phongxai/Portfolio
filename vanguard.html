Client

Vanguard Tech

I was asked to help the Junior Acheivement Program at Edison High School create a design for their app Vanguard tech. The app offers a platform for students to earn rewards with the goal of increasing student engagement and motivation.

Working with students from Edison, I came up with a sleek and minimal interface with emphasis on ease of use for both student and teacher. I also created a prototype website for the students to test and present.

Simple and Easy to use

Manage your points and use it to purchase goods from the school.
