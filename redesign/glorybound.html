Glorybound

  • Design at ADG
  • Front End Development at ADG

Website redesign for Glorybound, a non profit family restoration center that helps families of men and women who are struggling with alcohol and substance abuse based in Minneapolis, MN. Their previous website was outdated, so I was asked to redesign and develop their new site.

The newly revamped website is clean and minimal providing a better experience for users and a color scheme that would better portray Glorybound's organization. Powered by WordPress and built with custom HTML, CSS, and JQUERY.
