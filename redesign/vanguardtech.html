Vanguard tech

  • App Design
  • Prototype

I was asked to help the Junior Acheivement Program at Edison High School create a design for their app Vanguard tech. The app offers a platform for students to earn rewards with the goal of increasing student engagement and motivation.

Working with students from Edison, I came up with a sleek and minimal interface with emphasis on ease of use for both student and teacher. Due to time contraints, a volunteer developer decided to not move forward with the app, so I decided to create a prototype website for the students to test and present.
